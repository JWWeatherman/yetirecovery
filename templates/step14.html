Yeti Bitcoin Cold Storage

Step 11: Copy files to LARGE external hard drive.

  1. Connect you LARGE external hard drive.
  2. Open the Files application and check the box to display hidden files and folders.
  3. In the next step you will probably see a warning that Ubuntu can't copy symbolic links. This is fine so just click Skip All
  4. Navigate to your "Home" directory and copy these folders to your external hard drive:
    • .bitcoin
    • dpkg-repack
    • yeticold
